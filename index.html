Our Product

Wayang | Javanesse Puppet

Wayang is an Indonesia word for theatre. We create a handicraft which combines skills in using wood-based materials (Klepu wood, Medium Density Fibre board and the finest quality teak) with high quality print and fretwork.

Our products purpose is to awaken the legacy of Indonesian culture and introduce it to around the world, making Indonesian history more valuable and meaningful.

About Kitab

Kitab is an art and craft company borne out of the previous identity, de Kitab, established in 2010. Kitab is centralized in Surabaya, Indonesia, a well-known country rich in its heritage.

Kitab is a handicraft producer who combines skills in using wood-based materials (klepu wood, MDF, and finest quality teak) and also high quality print and fretwork. Our commitment is to continously explore the culture / legend of a region. Kitab is growing to be one of the world's most innovative companies within the art and craft industry.

Our philosophy is beyond legacy. Incessantly development of products and concepts have created an extraordinary artwork purposed to be a memento.
